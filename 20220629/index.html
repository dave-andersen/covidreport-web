Pennsylvania

Commentary

Cases have plateaued in Allegheny County and rose very slightly in the state, as did hospitalizations. ICU use has continued to drop a bit (it lags cases even more). Cases in NY, NJ, VA have been slowly rising, likely presaging a BA.5 driven slow rise in PA over the coming two weeks.
